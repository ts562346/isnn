About Us

A Faith-Centered Nigerian Muslim Community in Nova Scotia

We are Nigerians residing in Nova Scotia who share a profound love for Islam and a strong sense of responsibility toward its preservation and propagation. United by faith and a common heritage, we are committed to upholding the teachings of Islam in our daily lives while fostering a supportive and spiritually enriching community for our members.

Our collective aim is to strengthen our own understanding and practice of Islam and to actively transmit its values, principles, and teachings to the next generation through consistent learning, unity, and service.

Who we are

Our community is dedicated to nurturing an environment where our children and future generations can grow with a firm foundation in the Qur’an and Sunnah, embodying sound character and a strong Islamic identity.

Through continuous learning, community engagement, and the organization of Islamic programs and activities, we strive to foster a supportive and spiritually enriching environment for all members.

How we started

In 2018, a small group of families began regular gatherings of remembrance (adhkar), supplication, and encouragement upon righteousness and piety with sincerity for the sake of Allah (SWT).

By the grace of Allah, the circle expanded and became a platform for unity, learning, and spiritual upliftment for families across Nova Scotia.

In November 2020, the group was formally registered as a non-profit organization, enabling more structured programs, outreach, and educational initiatives.

Our Vision

To nurture a united and spiritually grounded Nigerian Muslim community in Nova Scotia, devoted to the teachings of Islam, exemplifying righteousness, and raising future generations firmly rooted in authentic Islamic values and knowledge.

Our Mission

To promote the understanding and practice of Islam among members through regular Islamic lectures, educational programs, and community engagement, fostering spiritual growth, unity, and moral excellence. We strive to equip our children and future generations with sound Islamic knowledge and character, guided by the Qur’an and Sunnah, so they may live as confident and responsible Muslims in society.
