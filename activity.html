Azkar Gathering

We organize Adhkar gatherings on a regular basis, scheduled for the first Sunday of every month. These spiritually enriching sessions provide members with the opportunity to come together in remembrance of Allah, engage in supplications, and strengthen their faith within a supportive community environment.

The gatherings are held in person at the Centre for Islamic Development Mosque, offering a conducive atmosphere for collective worship and reflection. In addition, provisions are made for members who are unable to attend physically to participate virtually via Zoom, ensuring inclusivity and broader engagement.

Through these consistent monthly programs, the Society aims to foster unity, spiritual growth, and a deeper connection to Islamic practices among its members.

Ramadan Iftar

The Society organizes a collective iftar during Ramadan, at which all members are encouraged to attend. The gathering is typically conducted as a potluck, complemented by Islamic lectures that enrich the spiritual experience of participants.

The event has consistently been engaging and rewarding. Members are advised to look out for the next scheduled iftar during the upcoming Ramadan, with further details to be provided on the “Upcoming Events” page.

Assistance to Nigerian International Students in Nova Scotia during Ramadan

The Society provides support to Nigerian international Muslim students during the month of Ramadan by offering packages of essential food items to those who express interest.

During this time, eligible students are encouraged to register their interest, after which arrangements will be made to contact them and deliver the packages during Ramadan. Photographs of previously distributed food items may be included here for reference.

Eid-Il-Adha Celebration

The Society organizes annual Eid al-Adha celebrations for its members. During this occasion, members come together in unity and joy to commemorate the festival.

The program includes the sharing of beneficial knowledge, interactive and educational activities—such as Islamic and Qur’anic quizzes conducted through Kahoot—and the delivery of insightful Islamic sermons to further enrich the spiritual experience of attendees.
