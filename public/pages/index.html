About ISNNS

Islamic Society of Nigerians in Nova Scotia

We are Nigerians in Nova Scotia committed to preserving and propagating Islam while nurturing future generations upon authentic Islamic values.

Our Vision

To nurture a united and spiritually grounded Nigerian Muslim community.

Our Mission

To strengthen Islamic learning through lectures, programs, and community engagement.

Founded through family adhkar gatherings in 2018 and formally registered as a non-profit in 2020.
